# Savera National Project: Financial Literacy Toolkit

## Overview
This toolkit provides interactive resources, graphs, and case studies to document and promote financial literacy across rural and urban slums in India.

## Data Sources & Citations

To assure validity and maintain a high standard of academic and non-profit integrity, the data representations in our charts and case studies are drawn directly from the following credible Indian macro-financial reports:

1. **PMJDY (Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana) Records:**
   - **Data Reference:** Year-wise Bank Account Access growth (2014-2024), scaling from roughly ~35% pre-2014 coverage to near ~80%+ effective coverage in poor demographics.
   - **Context:** Used in the "Year-wise Bank Account Access Coverage" area chart and the "Field Study" bar chart.

2. **NABARD (National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development) SHG-Bank Linkage Reports:**
   - **Data Reference:** Breakdown of initial loans going into Agriculture & Livestock versus Non-Farm SMEs.
   - **Context:** Used to accurately formulate the "Primary Loan Utilization Survey" Pie Chart constraints showing 42% in Agriculture vs 36% in Non-Farm enterprises.

3. **All India Debt & Investment Survey (AIDIS) / RBI Workpapers:**
   - **Data Reference:** Non-institutional credit (Moneylender dependence) dropping from over >50% down to sub-25% among active SHG participating demographics.
   - **Context:** Referenced in the field study stats regarding Moneylender Dependence.

4. **Global Findex Database (World Bank):**
   - **Data Reference:** Cross-referencing savings accumulations and generic account utilizations for Indian women.

## Case Studies
- **Kudumbashree & SEWA:** Referencing official state program metrics from Kerala and Gujarat respectively.
- **Bandhan & Ujjivan:** Sourced from their official transition filings to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) from NBFC-MFIs to Universal and Small Finance Banks.
